About

I’m an AEP Multi-Solution Senior Consultant at Adobe who helps organizations turn customer data into measurable marketing outcomes. I work at the intersection of strategy and delivery: translating business goals into technical roadmaps, data flows, and real-world experiences that marketing and analytics teams can trust every day.

My core focus is the Adobe Experience Cloud—including Adobe Experience Platform, Real-Time CDP, Journey Optimizer, Target, and Customer Journey Analytics—and how it fits alongside the Google Marketing Platform, especially Google Analytics. I care about clean, durable architecture: identity resolution, event and profile modeling, consent-aware data collection, API-driven activation, and tagging patterns that scale as stacks evolve.

On engagements, I partner with marketing, analytics, and IT from discovery through go-live: leading workshops, defining measurement frameworks, designing data collection and integration patterns, configuring platforms, testing end to end, and supporting hypercare so teams are confident running what we’ve built.

A key part of my role is owning the analytics strategy on client websites—designing tracking to reflect the full customer lifecycle, implementing and evolving Adobe and Google Analytics libraries, and collaborating with internal and external teams to define business requirements and the data captured via tag management systems. I regularly validate, debug, and troubleshoot implementations and stay proactive in identifying and resolving issues.

I’m motivated by outcomes: sharper audiences, faster experimentation, clearer attribution, and journeys that feel coherent to customers. I keep learning as the space shifts—new capabilities, privacy expectations, and integration patterns—and share perspectives through professional networks, including LinkedIn, where you’ll find the latest version of this story, recommendations, and activity.
